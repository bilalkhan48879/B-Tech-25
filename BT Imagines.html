How to Use BT Imagines

Welcome to BT Imagines, your free text-to-image generator! Here's how to use it:

  1. Enter a detailed description of the image you want to create in the text box.
  2. Click the "Generate Images" button.
  3. Wait while our AI creates 6 unique images based on your description.
  4. Once generated, you can download images with or without the BT Apps watermark.

Tips for best results:

  • Be specific in your descriptions.
  • Experiment with different phrasings to get varied results.
  • Have fun and let your imagination run wild!
